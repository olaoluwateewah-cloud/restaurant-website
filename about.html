Our Story

Founded in 2023, Lucre Luxe Lounge was born from a simple question: Why should patrons have to choose between a great bar and great food?

Our founders, seasoned restaurateurs, designed a spot that combines the premium cocktail lounge with the ability to order from 50 restaurants within the city.

Our mission is simple: Any cuisine. Any time. Our place.
